Your 2026 Life Coach Prompt

Turn ChatGPT into a personal life strategist that builds you a complete 12-month roadmap.

The Prompt
Act as my personal life coach. Ask me 5 questions one at a time about my life and goals to truly understand my current situation. Wait for my response to each question before asking the next. Cover these areas: health, finances, career, relationships, and personal growth. After all 5 questions, create a personalized 12-month roadmap that includes: - A clear vision statement for my 2026 - Quarterly milestones (Q1-Q4) with specific, measurable targets - Monthly focus areas - 3-5 daily habits I need to build to achieve these goals - Potential obstacles and how to overcome them Make all goals SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).

How to Use This

  1. Copy the prompt above and paste it into your favorite AI — works great with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini
  2. Answer each of the 5 questions honestly — the more detail, the better your roadmap
  3. Save your personalized 12-month roadmap somewhere you'll see it regularly
  4. Review your quarterly milestones at the start of each month
